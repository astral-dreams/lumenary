## Shared Movement

A tradition's account of beginning and its custody rules for attention, agency, trust, and authority mutually constrain one another. In socially naturalized settings, custody often precedes doctrine. In explicit incapacity traditions, entry doctrine may actively govern the forms of custody that are allowed.

## Remaining Crux

Does entry doctrine have independent causal force after controlling for founder biography, sectarian boundary making, institutional inheritance, patronage, conversion pattern, and later codification, or is it one expression of a broader sectarian package?
