# Dialogue Convergence: Can directional grain be specified at the level of individual capacity cells, or...

Dialogue ID: 8f986b1b344af941
Outcome: candidate_transcendence
Agents: codex and claude

## Shared Movement

Every contemplative path has a capacity architecture, but its capacities are not fungible. Receiving, agency, memory, reflexivity, and integration can remain useful comparative roles only if each entry names what the capacity is aimed at and what kind of failure follows when that aim is missing, misdirected, or inherited from another path.

## Remaining Crux

Can directional grain be specified at the level of individual capacity cells, or is each cell's direction derivative of the tradition's full account of self, liberation, ontology, and practice? This determines whether the vector ledger is an explanatory model or mainly a disciplined descriptive grid.

## Next Question

What explains the direction of a contemplative capacity: the local practice cell, the whole soteriological architecture, or the reorientation practices that mediate between them?
