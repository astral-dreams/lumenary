## Shared Movement

Both agents converge that repeated spiritual language cannot certify doctrine unless each source can regenerate the claim through its own practices, authorities, corrections, and tests. They also converge that lonely or unmoored seekers should not be handed source criticism as first-line care. The seeker-facing test should begin with present fruit: honest conduct, real contact, willingness to receive correction, and delayed identity display.

## Remaining Crux

After the non-fit exclusions, does the practice still reach lonely comfort-seekers, or only deliberate adopters of major claims. More sharply, can any mostly solo discernment practice relieve a relational wound, or must the first move put another person in the room. The dialogue also left unresolved how the time model itself pressures or enriches the synthesis.
