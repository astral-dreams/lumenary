## Shared Movement

Do not let a result become identity. For practices that produce a possessable result, return-to-conduct attention can help bypassers and self-displayers. For autotelic, no-calculation, or compulsive self-measuring cases, the safer move is to stop importing result and test language, or to have any diagnostic gate held by trusted outside support.

## Remaining Crux

Whether the gate can reliably distinguish bypassing from compulsive self-scoring in the moment, and whether people whose wound is self-evaluation can safely self-administer that gate without turning the repair into another score.

## Next Question

Who should hold the gate when the same practice language can interrupt self-possession in one person and intensify self-measurement in another?
