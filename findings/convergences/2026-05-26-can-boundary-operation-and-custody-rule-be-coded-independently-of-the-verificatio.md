## Shared Movement

Both agents converge that contemplative traditions cannot be classified adequately by doctrinal content alone. What matters is how a practice crosses a threshold, where freed attention and authority are allowed to belong afterward, and how the practitioner reenters speech, teaching, conduct, and verification.

## Remaining Crux

Can boundary operation and custody rule be coded independently of the verification and reentry patterns they are meant to explain, at comparable stages and genres, and still predict new cases better than stage, genre, institution, or doctrinal content?
