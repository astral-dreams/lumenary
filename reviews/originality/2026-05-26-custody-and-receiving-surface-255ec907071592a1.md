## Exact Claim

A contemplative path that weakens ordinary self-ownership needs both a custody architecture and a local human receiving surface. Custody names where transformation functions are assigned, such as causal process, hidden Self, teacher, vow, grace, community, ritual, God, or individual practitioner. Receiving surface names what must remain available in the practitioner, such as investigation, entrusting, recognition, loving attention, non-fabrication, or embodied presence. The strongest defensible version is a coding framework that tests whether receiving surface predicts failures, interventions, sequencing, and verification beyond custody assignment alone.

## Claim Units

- Self-weakening contemplative paths relocate transformation functions rather than simply deleting agency.
- Custody assignment can be coded separately from practitioner-side availability.
- Receiving surface may be an independent variable rather than only the local expression of custody.
- The model should predict failure modes, teacher interventions, sequencing, and verification practices.
- The claim is a comparative heuristic and test protocol, not a metaphysical claim about what causes liberation.

Structural observation: Tannisho strongly supports vow-held custody: Amida's vow carries salvific efficacy and self-power calculation is treated as deviation. Yet it still requires hearing, entrusting, teacher lineage, and restricted readiness, so a receiving surface remains visible. SN 22.59 negates ownership and mastery over the aggregates, but the discourse still depends on instruction, discernment, memory, and seeing with right understanding, so causal-process custody does not eliminate local investigation. Brihadaranyaka 3.7.23 gives the opposite residue policy: ordinary objectification fails, but the hidden knower or inner controller is authorized as the remainder. Dogen's practice-realization strains the model because practice and realization are not cleanly separable stages, making custody and receiving surface look like an analyst-imposed split. These primary texts support the distinction as a useful coding question, but not as a proven independent structure.

Secondary literature gap: This audit used accessible translations and open academic or practitioner-facing sources. It did not fully close-read Shinran's Kyogyoshinsho, Dogen scholarship, Dzogchen or Mahamudra manuals, John of the Cross, Teresa of Avila, Christian grace controversy sources, or Sanskrit, Pali, Japanese, Tibetan, and Latin originals. It also did not run practitioner interviews. Novelty should not rise without split-source coding and interview evidence.

Risk of overread: High. The model can absorb nearly any tradition by assigning a custom custody holder and custom receiving surface. It also risks translating devotional, ritual, lineage, and metaphysical claims into systems vocabulary that practitioners may reject. The key circularity risk is using warnings and repairs both to infer receiving surface and to confirm that receiving surface predicts warnings and repairs.

## Unlike Statement

Unlike Pargament's agency styles, Lindbeck's doctrine-as-grammar, Asad's discursive tradition, VCE adverse-effects research, upaya capacity pedagogy, Pure Land Other Power, Christian attention and grace theology, pointing-out instruction, and prior Lumenary ledgers, this idea says that the assigned holder of transformation and the practitioner's required mode of availability should be coded separately, then tested to see whether the second variable predicts warnings, sequencing, teacher repairs, and verification beyond the first.

## Anomaly Probe

Candidate: Dogen-style practice-realization, inherited communal practice, and radical Other Power cases where the analyst cannot cleanly separate custody, entry, and receiving surface.

Why it strains the model: If practice and realization are nondual, there may be no discrete landing event and no separate receiving surface. If a person is gradually formed by inherited communal participation before explicit assent, the receiving surface may be socialized rather than locally chosen. If radical Other Power refuses any self-power residue, calling entrusting a human surface may distort the doctrine by reintroducing the agency it rejects.

Confidence effect: Lowers confidence from candidate discovery to extended. The model needs analyst-frame-refused, no-threshold, inherited-practice, practice-realization, radical-other-power, and undecidable values before use.

## Falsifiable Predictions

- If: Receiving surface is not reducible to custody assignment. Then: Within the same custody class, such as vow-held, grace-held, or teacher-held paths, different receiving surfaces will correlate with different warning profiles and repair instructions. Entrusting failures should be repaired differently from recognition failures, investigation failures, or loving-attention failures. Weakens if: If custody assignment alone predicts warnings, sequencing, verification, and repairs as well as the two-variable model, receiving surface is redundant. Test surface: Split-source blind coding of Pure Land, Christian contemplation, Dzogchen or Mahamudra, Theravada insight, and Advaita manuals, followed by practitioner interviews.
- If: The same receiving surface can appear under different custody systems. Then: Recognition, entrusting, waiting, loving attention, non-fabrication, or investigation should appear in more than one custody grammar, while their risks shift according to custody. Weakens if: If each receiving surface is tradition-specific and never appears independently of one custody grammar, the model is a descriptive relabeling rather than a transferable structure. Test surface: Matched primary-text pairs and inter-rater coding across traditions.
- If: Receiving-surface mismatch predicts cross-training difficulty. Then: Practitioners trained in investigation-heavy paths should show predictable difficulty entering entrusting, waiting, or non-fabricating paths, and the reverse should also occur, beyond doctrine, teacher style, temperament, and clinical-risk variables. Weakens if: Smooth cross-training with no predictable mismatch pattern, or mismatch fully explained by social ecology, identity investment, trauma history, or teacher competence. Test surface: Dual-practitioner interviews and preregistered coding of switching and hybrid-practice cases.
- If: Teacher interventions will target the receiving surface, not only belief about custody. Then: Manuals and teachers will correct how the practitioner investigates, entrusts, recognizes, waits, loves, relaxes, or embodies, even when the official custody claim remains stable. Weakens if: If interventions mainly correct agency belief, ritual obedience, community conformity, or doctrinal interpretation without separable availability-mode corrections. Test surface: Manual coding and teacher interviews asking for concrete failed-student cases and repair instructions.

## Cross-Domain Prediction

Domain: Psychotherapy and behavior-change programs

Structural translation: Custody becomes the assigned change agent: client agency, therapist, medication, group, protocol, exposure mechanism, sponsor, or higher power. Receiving surface becomes participant availability: insight, compliance, surrender, emotional exposure, behavioral rehearsal, trust, somatic tolerance, or relational openness.

Prediction: Programs with the same custody assignment but different receiving surfaces will produce different dropout, resistance, and repair patterns. For example, therapist-guided treatments requiring exposure tolerance should fail and be repaired differently from therapist-guided treatments requiring cognitive insight or relational trust.

Would count against it: If change-agent assignment, diagnosis, severity, therapeutic alliance, treatment dose, and social support explain dropout and repair patterns without an independent availability-mode variable.
