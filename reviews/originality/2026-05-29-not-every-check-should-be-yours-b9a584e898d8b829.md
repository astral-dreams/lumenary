## Exact Claim

Post-practice checks are not automatically safe. The decisive safety variable is who holds the check today: the practitioner, another person, a rule, ordinary conduct, or no check. Bypassers may need a self-held conduct check, compulsive self-scorers may need external, rule-held, or suspended checking, and oscillators need state-based assignment rather than identity labels.

## Claim Units

- A humility, release, or return check can become harmful self-scoring.
- Failure mode should determine check holder.
- Bypassers need conduct accountability more than relief from checking.
- Compulsive self-scorers may need the diagnostic held outside the self or suspended.
- Oscillators require present-state routing, not fixed type assignment.
- The practice is only publishable for a clear cohort: stable high-achievement practitioners harmed by self-scoring.

## Unlike Statement

Unlike Ignatian direction, Pargament agency styles, VCE support mapping, ERP anti-reassurance rules, and the prior Lumenary Who Holds The Gate record, this candidate says that a post-practice check must first ask who should hold the diagnostic today, because the same check can repair bypassing and worsen achievement-contingent self-scoring. That distinction is useful, but it is mostly a renamed and narrower safety-routing heuristic.

## Anomaly Probe

Candidate: OCD, scrupulosity, reassurance seeking, abusive authority, and valid solitude.

Why it strains the model: The model says a compulsive self-scorer may need a teacher, peer, clinician, or rule to hold the check. In OCD or scrupulosity, the same transfer can become reassurance compulsion. In abusive communities, external holding becomes control. In valid solitude, outside holding can weaken needed self-trust.

Confidence effect: Lowers confidence in any public practice unless the codebook includes clinical exclusions, unsafe-authority detection, anti-reassurance rules, and a valid-solitude option.

## Falsifiable Predictions

- If: Stable high-achievement practitioners high in achievement-contingent self-worth will fare worse with self-held after-use checks than with externally held, rule-held, or suspended checks. Then: Compared with external or rule-held conditions, self-held checks increase rumination, shame, comparison, and repeated interpretation, while not improving next-day conduct. Weakens if: Self-held checks reduce rumination and improve conduct as well as or better than external, rule-held, or suspended checks. Test surface: Two-week randomized pilot using contingencies of self-worth, rumination scales, mood ratings, and concrete duty completion.
- If: Practitioners using insight to avoid responsibility need a self-held return-to-conduct check more than they need rest from checking. Then: In high-bypass, low-self-scoring participants, a single conduct check increases repair behavior and reduces avoidance without increasing shame. Weakens if: The conduct check does not improve behavior, or it increases self-scoring at the same rate as in the compulsive scorer group. Test surface: Screened practice pilot with spiritual bypassing, avoidance, self-worth contingency, and next-day conduct measures.
- If: Practice manuals and teachers should assign diagnostic custody differently by stage, risk, and support ecology. Then: Split-source coding of manuals predicts held-out warnings and repairs involving self, teacher, community, rule, conduct, or no-check options. Weakens if: All holder assignments are explained by institution type, teacher personality, or generic spiritual direction without added predictive gain from failure-mode coding. Test surface: Blind manual coding plus practitioner interviews using concrete misinterpretation and repair cases.

## Cross-Domain Prediction

Domain: Software review and knowledge work

Structural translation: The post-practice check becomes the post-output quality diagnostic. Depending on failure mode, quality can be held by the worker, a reviewer, acceptance criteria, a release rule, or temporarily not checked.

Prediction: High-performing workers whose worth is tied to quality scores will complete more and ruminate less when acceptance criteria or reviewers hold the check, while avoidant workers will need a self-held concrete completion check.

Would count against it: Generic code review, WIP limits, or ordinary management explain all outcome differences, or holder assignment by failure mode adds no predictive value.
