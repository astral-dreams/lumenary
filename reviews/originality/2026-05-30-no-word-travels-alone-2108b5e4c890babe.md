## Exact Claim

A spiritual word travels with practice, authority, verification, and the receiver's wound; therefore translation-strain work must test both source-side role and receiver-side misuse before a borrowed term becomes a responsible teaching.

## Claim Units

- A spiritual term is not portable by dictionary meaning alone.
- Terms carry training practices, correction authorities, verification rules, and forms of conduct.
- A modern receiver's loneliness, shame, ambition, fear, or visibility hunger can counterfeit the term's use.
- Detachment, surrender, emptiness, witness, humility, and service can become withdrawal, passivity, nihilism, self-erasure, or performance identity.
- Human-condition fit is clearer than in many comparison claims: it targets loneliness, digital comparison, withdrawal, meaning loss, and achievement-contingent self-worth in stable supported readers, not acute crisis.

## Unlike Statement

Unlike Wittgenstein, Asad, Lindbeck, speech-act theory, spiritual bypassing, and VCE context research, this candidate says a borrowed spiritual term must pass a two-sided gate: source role plus receiver-wound misuse. That combination is useful for Lumenary practice design, but most parts are known.

## Anomaly Probe

Candidate: Living traditions that safely transmit difficult words through institution, ritual, teacher relationship, and community without asking each receiver to analyze wounds.

Why it strains the model: If a novice learns detachment, surrender, emptiness, or service through embodied correction rather than private receiver-wound reflection, the proposed sheet may be an outsider's psychologizing grid. It could weaken trust, increase self-monitoring, or miss the actual correction holder.

Confidence effect: Confidence remains moderate. Receiver-wound coding should be a risk triage for extracted, online, therapeutic, or self-synthesis contexts, not a universal condition for all religious language.

## Falsifiable Predictions

- If: Receiver wounds create predictable distortions of the same borrowed term. Then: Detachment skews toward withdrawal in lonely or avoidant participants, but toward disciplined non-reactivity in supported overattached participants. Service skews toward self-erasure in achievement-contingent participants, but toward concrete repair in stable participants. Weakens if: Term misuse is not predicted by receiver wound after controlling for teacher quality, community support, mental health, and prior practice. Test surface: Pre-registered cohort study coding wound profile, term use, action taken, and follow-up conduct.
- If: The Word Carry Test improves conduct more than ordinary reflection for the target cohort. Then: Compared with ordinary journaling, stable users produce more concrete accountable actions, fewer avoidance rationalizations, and no increase in shame or rumination. Weakens if: Ordinary journaling, values clarification, or a simple action plan performs as well or better, or the practice increases checking, shame, or self-surveillance. Test surface: Randomized pilot with three arms: Word Carry Test, ordinary journaling, and action-plan control.
- If: Source role plus receiver wound predicts practitioner warnings better than source role alone. Then: Teachers and spiritual directors name different warnings for the same word depending on loneliness, shame, ambition, trauma history, social support, and online visibility pressure. Weakens if: Practitioners say tradition, teacher style, social ecology, and clinical risk fully explain the warning profile without a receiver-wound field. Test surface: Interview study asking for concrete misuse and repair cases, not agreement with the theory.
- If: Online extraction intensifies word misuse when correction holders are absent. Then: Public posts using terms such as surrender, emptiness, witness, boundaries, service, or humility without practice source or correction holder show higher rates of avoidance, grandiosity, withdrawal, or performance identity markers. Weakens if: Misuse rates are unrelated to correction-holder absence or are better predicted only by platform incentives and mental-health variables. Test surface: Corpus coding of spiritual, therapeutic, and self-help social media posts with blinded behavioral-risk tags.

## Cross-Domain Prediction

Domain: workplace and therapy vocabulary

Structural translation: Terms such as boundaries, resilience, trauma-informed, accountability, psychological safety, and self-care also travel with practices, authorities, verification norms, and receiver wounds.

Prediction: When these words are imported without trained practice and correction holders, different wounds will counterfeit them predictably: boundaries becomes avoidance, resilience becomes overwork, safety becomes control, accountability becomes shame ritual.

Would count against it: Definitions alone transfer the terms safely across cohorts, and misuse does not vary by receiver wound, correction holder, or institutional practice.
