The method-authority frontier has repeatedly generated the same two-axis result, so the competent next move is consolidation, freezing new generation, and reopening only on a named trigger: new source class, real anomaly, practitioner challenge, failed merge, or the test of whether method-type predicts verification and reentry better than tradition membership.

## Unlike Statement

Unlike Kanban WIP limits, GTD, theoretical saturation, Lakatosian degeneration, salami slicing, rumination research, and the prior Lumenary More Is Not The Same As Progress record, this idea's distinct claim is that a method studying post-method release can itself fail to release by repeatedly restating its own result. That recursive mirror is rhetorically sharp, but the operational rule is mostly already known.

## Anomaly Probe

Candidate: Legitimate repetition: liturgy, zazen, nembutsu, deliberate teaching restatement, iterative writing, independent convergence, and hard research that needs multiple framings before the real variable appears.

Why it strains the model: The model risks treating all restatement as non-release. Some traditions and disciplines use repeated forms because repetition is the practice, trains conduct, or reveals differences not visible in the first pass. In research, repeated independent derivations can increase confidence or uncover subclaims. A gate could suppress anomalies, silence hard work, or become avoidance by people afraid to continue.

Confidence effect: Keeps novelty low and makes the claim publishable only as a bounded production-control rule. It must include reopen criteria and an exception for distinct testable content.
