## Exact Claim

A silence or blank state cannot authorize its own meaning; the trustworthy evidence is in the disciplined return: prior instruction, correction after the event, visible conduct, and care for remaining duties and relationships.

## Claim Units

- Silence, blankness, or quiet is not self-authenticating evidence of Self, no-self, attainment, worth, or failure.
- Brihadaranyaka and early Buddhist texts disagree about what remains after negation, but both require more than private blankness alone.
- Return discipline, instruction, correction, conduct, and ordinary duty, should be the first audit layer.
- The target cohort is stable self-directed practitioners and high-striving people who convert inner states into worth verdicts.
- The proposed practice delays self, no-self, attainment, failure, and worth claims for twenty-four hours.

## Unlike Statement

Unlike Silence Needs Correction, Ignatian discernment, Jamesian fruits, Alstonian overriders, VCE support routing, and prior Lumenary return-test records, this idea says a blank should be judged first by the ecology of return, especially for people who turn inner quiet into a worth verdict. The cohort narrowing is useful, but the core claim is mostly renamed prior art.

## Anomaly Probe

Candidate: Direct recognition, clear-light sleep, or stable solitary realization cases where mature practitioners report immediate knowing with ethical integration and no explicit return-discipline gate, plus the opposite anomaly of abusive or compulsive correction.

Why it strains the model: If some mature sources and practitioners treat silence or recognition as self-evident in a bounded way, the claim that no silence proves itself is too broad. If correction commonly produces shame, dependence, or coercion, return discipline cannot be presented as a universal first layer.

Confidence effect: Lowers novelty and practice confidence. The model survives as a narrow safety-routing heuristic for isolated overinterpreters and achievement-contingent practitioners, not as a general doctrine.

Then: Blind coders can identify instruction-before, correction-after, conduct-test, relationship-duty, or no-return-discipline from one source layer and predict held-out warnings or repairs better than tradition label alone. Weakens if: Coders need outcome passages to identify return discipline, or tradition label and teacher style predict warnings equally well. Test surface: Retrospective VCE-style case coding and a prospective support-routing pilot.
