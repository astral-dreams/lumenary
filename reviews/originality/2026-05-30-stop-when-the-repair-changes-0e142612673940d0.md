## Exact Claim

A borrowed spiritual sentence is not ready to teach because its meaning seems to fit. It is ready only if the repair target, authority, verification, and ordinary-life action survive the crossing. If the repair changes, it must be named and tested as a new medicine.

## Claim Units

- Semantic similarity is not enough for responsible teaching transfer.
- A comparison must ask whether the same wounded habit or practical problem is being corrected.
- Authority and verification must not be silently replaced by the borrower.
- The borrowed sentence must produce checkable ordinary conduct, not only private certainty.
- If the repair task changes, the result is a new teaching proposal rather than shared wisdom.

The overlap is a move away from ordinary object-self language, but the repair differs: one stabilizes an authority-bearing self, the other loosens clinging to anything as self. Dao De Jing 48 supports subtractive comparison, while MN 1 warns that the analyst can keep reintroducing hidden possession through the grammar of conceiving.

Risk of overread: High. The model risks forcing traditions into modern therapeutic repair language. Pure Land nembutsu, Dzogchen pointing-out, sacramental speech, Dogen practice-realization, communal prayer, and ritual duty may function through vow, grace, recognition, participation, or formation rather than a named wound. Human-condition fit is real but narrow: stable unsupported seekers facing meaning loss, digital comparison, loneliness, and achievement-contingent certainty. It is not fit for acute crisis, dissociation, psychosis, mania, severe depression, OCD or scrupulosity loops, addiction withdrawal, unsafe authority, or medical blackout concerns.

## Unlike Statement

Unlike Panikkar, Freiberger, Smith, Wittgenstein, Asad, spiritual bypassing, and VCE context research, this candidate says a borrowed spiritual sentence must be downgraded from convergence to new teaching whenever the repair target changes. That is a useful Lumenary gate, but most of its parts are established prior art.

## Anomaly Probe

Candidate: Pure Land nembutsu and Dogen practice-realization.

Why it strains the model: These may not be best understood as tools that repair a named modern wound. Nembutsu can function as entrusting gratitude under Other Power, and Dogen can refuse a separable tool-result model. Forcing them through a repair-survival gate may distort the native practice before it protects the borrower.

Confidence effect: Lowers confidence in universal scope. The model should be a teaching-transfer safety gate for extracted sentences, not a general theory of all spiritual language.

## Falsifiable Predictions

- If: Repair continuity adds information beyond semantic role similarity. Then: In the Atman and anatta case, coders will find partial role-similarity in de-objectifying ordinary identity but different repair targets, verification norms, authority holders, and final practice aims. Weakens if: Dual-trained practitioners and scholars judge the repair to be the same after authority, verification, and practice aim are made explicit. Test surface: Blind coding of paired primary passages with fields for role, repair target, authority, verification, conduct, and final aim.
- If: Borrowed terms with repair mismatch produce predictable misuse. Then: Witness, emptiness, surrender, detachment, and no-self will skew toward withdrawal, passivity, nihilism, private certainty, or self-erasure when used without their original correction holders. Weakens if: Misuse is unrelated to repair mismatch after controlling for teacher support, community, mental-health risk, and prior practice. Test surface: Pre-registered cohort study of stable cross-tradition readers, coding borrowed term, named wound, action taken, and follow-up conduct.
- If: The Repair Check changes practice outcomes for the target cohort. Then: Compared with ordinary journaling, stable supported users produce more concrete contact, duty completion, truth-telling, repair action, or rest without display, with no increase in shame or rumination. Weakens if: Ordinary journaling, source checking, trusted conversation, or a simple action plan performs as well or better, or the check increases isolation and self-surveillance. Test surface: Randomized pilot with Repair Check, ordinary journaling, and action-plan control arms.
- If: The repair-survival field changes comparative scoring decisions. Then: A translation-strain team will downgrade some apparent convergences after adding repair target, authority, verification, and ordinary conduct fields. Weakens if: Scores do not change beyond what Panikkar, Freiberger, Smith, spiritual-bypass screening, and current Lumenary word-role records already produce. ## Cross-Domain Prediction

Domain: Psychotherapy and organizational language.

Structural translation: Terms such as boundaries, resilience, trauma-informed, accountability, psychological safety, and exposure carry repair targets, authority, verification, and expected conduct from their source settings.

Prediction: When these terms are borrowed without preserving the repair target and correction holder, misuse will vary predictably: boundaries becomes avoidance, resilience becomes overwork, safety becomes control, accountability becomes shame ritual, and exposure becomes coercion.

Would count against it: Definitions alone transfer the terms safely across contexts, and misuse does not vary by repair mismatch, authority replacement, or receiver wound.
