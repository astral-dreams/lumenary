## Exact Claim

Low translation strain cannot strengthen a cross-tradition convergence claim until provenance has been checked, because smooth likeness can mark borrowing, shared translators, shared lineage, or modern universalizing rather than independent recurrence.

## Claim Units

- Independent convergence requires that borrowing, inheritance, shared milieu, translator mediation, and modern universalizing be unlikely or explicitly controlled.
- Low lexical or logical strain is ambiguous. It can indicate stable recurrence, but it can also indicate that the words or arguments traveled.
- The Gaudapada and Madhyamaka non-origination pair is a low-strain case with strong historical contact pressure, so it cannot count as two independent witnesses.
- The Plotinus and Daodejing return pair is a higher-strain and less clearly connected pair, so it is a better test of rough independent recurrence than of smooth agreement.
- Human-condition fit is narrow: the idea serves meaning loss and loneliness in unaffiliated seekers who use all-traditions-agree as borrowed certainty, but it does not supply community by itself.

## Unlike Statement

Unlike standard comparative-method warnings and the prior Lumenary independence field, this idea says a strain score has no evidential direction until provenance is coded; under plausible contact, smooth likeness should often reduce independent-confirmation confidence. That is a useful extension, not a discovery.

## Anomaly Probe

Candidate: Borrowing that increases rather than decreases strain, especially Chinese Buddhist matching-concepts cases and Gaudapada's Vedantic redeployment of Madhyamaka logic.

Why it strains the model: If borrowed terms are reinterpreted inside a new metaphysics, borrowing can produce low lexical strain and high doctrinal strain at the same time. A single inversion, smooth means borrowed and rough means independent, is therefore too crude.

Confidence effect: Keeps confidence moderate. Replace the one-axis inversion with layer-resolved coding for lexical, logical, ritual, authority, and metaphysical strain.

## Falsifiable Predictions

- If: Low lexical or logical strain is often a contact signal when traditions could historically interact. Then: In a coded sample, the lowest lexical and argument-structure strain pairs cluster in historically connected cases, such as Gaudapada and Madhyamaka, Christian apophasis and Neoplatonism, Sufism and Neoplatonism, or Daoist terms in early Chinese Buddhist translation. Weakens if: Low-strain pairs are distributed independently of contact after controlling for later translation choices and genre. Test surface: Blind dataset of 60 cross-tradition pairs coded for provenance and strain layer before convergence strength is scored.
- If: Independent recurrence is usually rougher than borrowed agreement. Then: Pairs with no plausible contact preserve role similarity while differing more strongly in vocabulary, authority, verification, and metaphysics. Weakens if: Well-attested independent pairs repeatedly show low strain across vocabulary, logic, authority, and practice aim. Test surface: Matched comparison of independent, contact, and uncertain-provenance pairs, scored by separate historians and practitioners.
- If: The provenance gate changes actual Lumenary scoring decisions. Then: Blind reviewers using translation strain alone promote more convergence claims than reviewers forced to code provenance first. Weakens if: The provenance gate changes no scores or only duplicates scholar intuition already captured by existing strain fields. Test surface: A/B coding trial on recent Lumenary convergence records.
- If: The Borrowed-Word Check helps a narrow seeker cohort without replacing community. Then: Stable unaffiliated seekers report less false certainty from all-paths-agree claims, similar or better curiosity, and no rise in loneliness or cynicism compared with ordinary source checking. Weakens if: The practice increases isolation, corrosive doubt, or performs no better than asking for citations. Test surface: Two-week non-clinical pilot with loneliness, certainty, cynicism, and community-contact measures.
