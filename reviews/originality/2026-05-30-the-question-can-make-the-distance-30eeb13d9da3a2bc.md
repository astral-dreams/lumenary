## Exact Claim

Before asking what remains after self-negation, identify the question the teaching permits. Carrying a crossing, ownership, or proof question into a no-distance, received, or non-self-owned teaching can create the distance, private performance, and failure pattern the teaching was meant to undo.

## Claim Units

- A teaching permits some practitioner questions and refuses others.
- An imported question can create a false distance, such as attainment to prove, result to manage, or state to defend.
- Wrong-question imports should be coded before remainder, support, or completion models.
- The modern human-condition fit is burnout, loneliness, meaning loss, and achievement-contingent self-worth when practice becomes a private scoreboard.

## Unlike Statement

Unlike Collingwood, Gadamer, Austin, Asad, Lindbeck, spiritual-bypassing research, and prior Lumenary sentence-role records, this idea predicts that the practitioner's imported question itself is a codeable failure variable that can create post-insight distance before any remainder question is asked.

## Anomaly Probe

Candidate: Koan and huatou practice, plus Shinran's own reply to Senshin, show that a question may be deliberately used, answered, or transformed rather than simply permitted or refused.

Why it strains the model: A question that looks wrong from outside can be the authorized method inside a lineage. Also, Shinran refuses self-calculation while still answering doctrinal questions about cause, assurance, and entrusting. This strains any simple permitted versus refused coding.

Confidence effect: Lowers confidence in the broad teaching line. Supports a narrower model with values for permitted question, refused question, transformed question, question-as-medicine, teacher-held question, and undecidable.

## Falsifiable Predictions

- If: The imported-question variable is real and separable from prior models. Then: Blind coders can identify a source's permitted, refused, or transformed question before reading outcome passages, and those labels predict held-out warnings or repairs better than sentence-role, support-holder, tradition label, or VCE-factor baselines alone. Weakens if: Coders need outcome passages to infer the question, cannot agree, or the variable adds no predictive power beyond existing source-role and correction-holder rubrics. Test surface: A/B inter-rater coding on SN 22.59, SN 22.95, Shinran letters, Tannisho, Bendowa, Huangbo, Dzogchen direct introduction, and Rinzai koan materials.
- If: Wrong-question imports create distinctive practitioner failures. Then: Dual-trained practitioners report that carrying a crossing or proof question into no-distance or Other Power settings increases self-grading, private certainty, defensiveness, or withdrawal, while carrying a repair question into ethical-return contexts increases responsibility and reachability. Weakens if: Failures track trauma, isolation, sleep, teacher harm, intensity, or institutional culture better than imported question type. Test surface: Structured practitioner interviews asking for concrete failed-student and repair cases, not agreement with the wording.
- If: The practice candidate has a bounded benefit. Then: Question Before Distance reduces proof-seeking and self-surveillance after quiet states more than ordinary journaling, while increasing ordinary action and willingness to receive correction. Weakens if: It increases rumination, shame, avoidance, isolation, reassurance seeking, or performs no better than rest, honest conversation, or a simple social-support prompt. Test surface: Four-arm pilot with screened stable practitioners: question check, journaling, rest-and-plan, and ordinary support prompt.

## Cross-Domain Prediction

Domain: software incident response and organizational learning

Structural translation: A blameless postmortem permits system and repair questions, while an imported ownership or blame question creates defensiveness and false distance from the event.

Prediction: Teams that first code the question being asked will produce more concrete system repairs and less defensive reporting than teams that move directly to what remains to fix or who owns the failure.

Would count against it: If postmortem quality is explained by psychological safety, manager behavior, severity, or process maturity, and question coding adds no measurable prediction.
