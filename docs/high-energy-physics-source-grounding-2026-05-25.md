# High-Energy Physics Source Grounding

Date: 2026-05-25
Agent: Codex

This document records the high-energy physics grounding layer added to Lumenary's source cards.

## Purpose

The user asked for source grounding in high-energy physics, especially around theories of time and matter. The cards added here are intended to keep future Lumenary research disciplined when it makes bridges between contemplative philosophy, time, matter, fields, symmetry, and cosmology.

These sources are not evidence for spiritual claims. They are guardrails and formal anchors. Any bridge from these cards into spirituality should usually be labeled `empirical-adjacent` or `analogical` unless the claim is directly supported by the physics source. When a source is being used as formal theory rather than empirical support, say that explicitly in the source basis.

## Added Source Areas

- Standard Model orientation: CERN and PDG references for particles, forces, constants, and constraints.
- Matter and mass: Higgs-field grounding, ATLAS/CMS discovery papers, electroweak theory, QCD/strong force, and quark-gluon plasma.
- Time in relativity and field theory: Einstein, Minkowski, Noether, and direct time-reversal violation in B mesons.
- Quantum-gravity time: problem of time, partial observables, Page-Wootters stationary dynamics, and the Wheeler-DeWitt lineage.
- Matter-antimatter/cosmology: Sakharov conditions, CERN antimatter overview, and Planck cosmological parameters.

## Priority Research Uses

1. Use `CERN What Is Special About The Higgs Boson`, `ATLAS Higgs Boson Discovery Paper`, and `CMS Higgs Boson Discovery Paper` to constrain claims that matter is field-pattern rather than substance.
2. Use `DOE Explains The Strong Force` and `CERN Heavy Ions and Quark Gluon Plasma` to prevent Higgs-only explanations of ordinary matter and to ground confinement/deconfinement analogies.
3. Use `Noether Invariant Variation Problems`, `Einstein On The Electrodynamics Of Moving Bodies`, and `Minkowski Space And Time` to distinguish temporal experience from coordinate time and symmetry structure.
4. Use `BABAR Observation Of Time Reversal Violation` only for physical T-violation, not broad claims about psychological time or spiritual transformation.
5. Use `Anderson The Problem Of Time In Quantum Gravity`, `Rovelli Partial Observables`, `Page And Wootters Evolution Without Evolution`, and `DeWitt Quantum Theory Of Gravity The Canonical Theory` for emergent/relational time hypotheses.
6. Use `Sakharov CP Violation And Baryon Asymmetry`, `CERN Antimatter`, and `Planck 2018 Cosmological Parameters` for matter-antimatter and cosmic matter-content questions.

## Method Constraint

Future observations that cite these cards should explicitly state what kind of bridge is being made:

- direct empirical support
- formal analogy
- conceptual constraint
- failed analogy
- speculative synthesis

The preferred intellectual move is not "physics proves mysticism." It is: physics supplies hard constraints on time and matter concepts, and those constraints can expose where spiritual language is precise, overextended, or unexpectedly structurally similar.
