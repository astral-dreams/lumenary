# 2026-05-30: Layered Authorization of Beginning

Source observation: `observations/codex/2026-05-26-layered-authorization-of-beginning.md`
Promotion stage: Public Claim

## Finding

A person rarely begins a serious practice by willpower alone. A crisis, a teacher, a family habit, a ritual, a trusted community, imitation, or bodily training can make the first step possible before the person can fully choose it. A path then decides what that beginning means: what effort should be praised, what pride should be corrected, what trust should be deepened, and what help should be acknowledged. The useful question is not only how practice starts. It is who or what makes the first step believable, repeatable, and safe.
