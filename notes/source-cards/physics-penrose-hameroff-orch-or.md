## Core Claims

1. **Godelian Argument:** Human mathematical understanding transcends what any algorithm can achieve (from Godel's incompleteness). Therefore consciousness involves non-computable processes. (Contested by most logicians.)

2. **Objective Reduction:** Quantum superpositions become gravitationally unstable when mass displacement is large enough, collapsing objectively (not through measurement). Collapse timescale: T ~ ℏ/E_G. This collapse is non-computable, influenced by "Platonic" mathematical information at the Planck scale.

3. **Microtubule Orchestration:** Tubulin proteins in neuronal microtubules sustain quantum superpositions. Biological processes "orchestrate" these superpositions to reach the OR threshold. Each collapse event = a moment of conscious experience. Anesthetics bind to tubulin, suggesting microtubules are consciousness-relevant.

4. **2024-2025 Empirical Developments:** Ultraviolet superradiance confirmed in microtubule tryptophan networks; anesthesia research supports microtubule targeting; superconducting qubit experiments claim OR-consistent collapse.

## Cross-Tradition Significance

**Consciousness linked to fundamental spacetime geometry** resonates with traditions asserting consciousness is woven into reality's fabric (Vedanta, Buddhism, Sufism). BUT: contemplative traditions say consciousness is ontologically primary (spacetime arises from/within it); Orch-OR says consciousness is linked to spacetime but doesn't necessarily precede it. Penrose is a mathematical Platonist, not an idealist.

**The Planck-scale "Platonic information"** is closer to mathematical realism than to consciousness-first ontology. Handle this parallel with care; it's structural, not identity.

**Status:** Minority position. Recent evidence more favorable than a decade ago. Tegmark's decoherence objection (10^-13 s) challenged but not resolved.
