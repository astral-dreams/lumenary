## Core Claims

1. **Variational Free Energy Minimization:** Any self-organizing system that maintains structural integrity must minimize free energy; an information-theoretic bound on surprise. Minimizing free energy = maximizing evidence for your internal model (self-evidencing). Two strategies: perceptual inference (update model) or active inference (change the world).

2. **Markov Blankets as Self:** A statistical boundary separating internal from external states. Sensory states (influenced by external) + active states (influenced by internal). The blanket IS the boundary of the "self." Cell membranes, organism surfaces, and mind-world interfaces are all Markov blankets. The self is not a substance but a statistical boundary; a pattern of conditional independence.

3. **Active Inference:** Organisms act on the world to make it match their predictions/preferences, not just passively update beliefs. Planning, imagination, and goal-directed behavior are free energy minimization over different time horizons.

## Key Connection: Meditation as Precision Relaxation

Laukkonen et al. (2021) "From many to (n)one" provides the most rigorous FEP-meditation bridge:

- **Focused attention meditation** = increasing precision weighting on one channel, suppressing prediction errors elsewhere
- **Open monitoring** = relaxing precision weighting across all channels; loosening priors, sampling the prediction landscape more freely
- **Non-dual/deconstructive meditation** = relaxing the self-model itself; the highest-level prior that organizes all predictions. Potentially dissolving the Markov blanket between "self" and "world"

## Cross-Tradition Significance

**FEP and Anatta:** Under FEP, the "self" is a generative model; a set of predictions, not a substance. Resonates profoundly with Buddhist anatta (no-self as construct, not substance). Buddhist dependent origination (contact → feeling → craving → clinging → becoming) maps onto predictive processing (prior → prediction → error → update).

**FEP and Zuowang/Five Sheaths:** The progressive dissolution of the self-model in deconstructive meditation (loosening priors at increasingly deep levels) is the FEP formalization of what the Vivekachudamani describes as pancha kosha negation and what Zhuangzi describes as zuowang.

**Criticisms:** May be unfalsifiable or trivially true (any persistent system fits). Markov blankets may be observer-relative statistical descriptions, not intrinsic features (Bruineberg et al.). Buddhism-FEP connection risks anachronism; different levels of description.
