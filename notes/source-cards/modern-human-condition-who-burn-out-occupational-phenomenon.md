## Notes

World Health Organization definition of burn-out as a workplace phenomenon involving exhaustion, mental distance or cynicism, and reduced professional efficacy. Use as grounding for teachings and practices about work, effort, responsibility, recovery, and the danger of spiritualizing overwork.
