## Original Claim

Cross-tradition likeness should be read on two separate evidential channels rather than one. Code the full reception profile of a pair (lexical, logical, metaphysical, authority, practice-aim, and verification strain, plus observed transformation after adoption), then score against two distinct targets: independent recurrence, for which plausible historical contact is a hard veto and only genuinely isolated pairs can be positives; and portability or resilience, for which transformed-contact pairs are the positives because a role survived re-embedding into a different ecology. Provenance is not retired but reassigned: it vetoes the two-witness inference and is irrelevant to portability. The error to prevent is no longer only smoothness mistaken for convergence; it is resilience reported under an independent-confirmation label.

## Why It Might Be New

Standard comparative method, source criticism, historical linguistics, and the constructivist critique of perennialism already warn that shared vocabulary may be transmission rather than independent witness. What is fresh is the positive move: rather than discarding contact cases as contamination, it treats transformed borrowing as a measurable second kind of evidence (portability), formally separated from independent recurrence so the two cannot be conflated on a single convergence axis. This converts the warning into a two-target scoring discipline aimed at downstream prediction of safeguards and beginner warnings.

## Critique

The distinction may be conceptually clean but operationally unstable: no-contact status is rarely absolute, reception histories are unevenly documented, and portability is often inferred from the same evidence used to deny independent recurrence, so the two labels may not be assignable independently. If blind coding cannot separate them, the model reduces to a refined vocabulary for uncertainty and only resilience remains measurable. Novelty also stays modest because much of the underlying caution is inherited from existing comparative scholarship.

## Next Research Directions

- Build the proposed 60-pair dataset with reception coding and blind, preregistered labels for independent recurrence and portability; require intercoder reliability before the distinction is used to guide doctrine or practice.
- Test whether transformed-contact cases predict portability outcomes (durable safeguards, beginner warnings, safe practice adaptations) without predicting independent recurrence; if both load together, drop the convergence channel and score only resilience.
- Code lexical, logical, metaphysical, authority, practice-aim, and verification strain separately for the Gaudapada and Madhyamaka pair to confirm the case is barred from the recurrence channel yet informative on the portability channel.
- Run a four-week human-condition trial: A/B the one-step Borrowed-Word Check against a two-step version that adds a belonging action, on stable unaffiliated seekers, measuring intellectual honesty, loneliness, anxiety, and corrosive doubt.
- Subordinate the model to a decision rule: code provenance and reception only at the resolution the actual decision requires (doctrine confidence, practice design, beginner warning, or pastoral care), and verify the simplified public rule loses nothing essential.
