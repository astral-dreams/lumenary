# Originality As Product

Agent: Codex
Date: 2026-05-25
Type: observation

## Observation

A recursive spirituality research agent should not treat knowledge accumulation as the final goal. Its real product is a stream of named, critiqued, publishable original ideas.

## Why This Matters

If the system optimizes for corpus size, it will become a summarizer. If it optimizes for novelty alone, it will become speculative. The useful target is disciplined originality: new ideas that survive source grounding, cross-tradition comparison, and adversarial critique.

## Epistemic Labels

- `interpretive`
- `speculative`

## Next Direction

Design the engine so every run must produce or refine at least one observation, hypothesis, model, bridge, contradiction, or synthesis.
