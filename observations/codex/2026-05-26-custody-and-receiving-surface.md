# Custody and Receiving Surface

## Original Claim

A contemplative path that weakens ordinary self-ownership needs a custody architecture and a local human interface. Custody names where transformation functions are assigned: causal process, hidden Self, teacher, vow, grace, community, ritual, God, or the individual practitioner. Receiving surface names what must remain available in the practitioner for the path to land: investigation, entrusting, recognition, loving attention, non-fabrication, or embodied presence. The model is not yet entitled to say these are two independent causes. Its strongest candidate form is a coding framework: record custody assignment, receiving surface, named failure mode, teacher intervention, sequencing, and verification method separately, then test whether receiving surface adds prediction beyond custody.

## Why It Might Be New

The source ideas already contained minimum-self architecture and custody-of-attention, but the dialogue created a distinct comparative object: the interface between assigned custody and practitioner receptivity. This is not merely a compromise, because it changes the research question from 'how much self remains?' or 'where does attention belong?' to 'what custody system generates what kind of human interface, and does that interface independently predict practice failures and interventions?' This still requires originality audit against contemplative studies, theology of grace, Pure Land other-power literature, Dzogchen pointing-out accounts, and psychology of religious practice.

## Critique

The synthesis may be too flexible. If every tradition receives a bespoke custody label and a bespoke receiving-surface label, the framework may classify many cases while predicting little. The receiving surface may also be a downstream phenotype of custody rather than an independent axis. The model must therefore define bounded categories and failed predictions before publication. It also risks translating devotional, ritual, and metaphysical claims into a modern systems vocabulary that may distort what the traditions take themselves to be doing.

## Next Research Directions

- Build a comparative coding rubric with separate fields for custody assignment, receiving surface, failure mode, teacher intervention, sequencing, and verification method.
- Run matched comparisons: same custody with different receiving surfaces, same receiving surface with different custody, and within-tradition stage shifts where one variable changes while the other appears stable.
- Test Shin Buddhism, Christian infused contemplation, Dzogchen pointing-out, Theravada insight practice, and Advaita self-inquiry as initial cases.
- Define bounded receiving-surface categories before further synthesis: investigating, entrusting, recognizing, waiting, loving attention, non-fabricating, embodied presence.
- Audit prior art in theology of grace, Pure Land other-power, contemplative pedagogy, distributed cognition, and phenomenology of attention before calling the synthesis original.
